The problem

Traditional insulation makes homes choose between comfort and appearance.

Plastic film kits look temporary, bulky drapes block natural light, and single-season materials create waste. Window Scene is built for homeowners who want efficiency without making a room feel unfinished.

01

Year-round energy loss

Windows leak up to 25–30% of heating and cooling energy. Heat escapes in winter, and in summer the sun overheats rooms and overworks the AC.

02

Temporary-looking fixes

Existing kits solve one problem while creating another: a room that feels wrapped, not designed.

03

Seasonal waste

Reusable panels support a longer-lasting, more intentional approach to home efficiency.

The solution

Insulation that looks intentional.

Window Scene combines a practical thermal layer with decorative surfaces designed for seasonal swaps, privacy, ambiance, and branded spaces.

How it's built

Engineered as a layered panel.

Each panel stacks a decorative printed face, an insulating core, and a reflective barrier that works both ways — keeping heat out in summer and in during winter — onto renter-friendly magnetic mounts.
